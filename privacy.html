Privacy Statement

When we are a processor, the privacy statement of the relevant controller on whose behalf we process will apply.

When we process your personal information as a data controller, Anchorpoint Technology. (referred to in this Privacy Statement as “Anchorpoint,” “us,” “we,” “our”) is the controller of your personal information and contact details are provided below under the heading "How to Contact US"

Anchorpoint has certified certain Services under the E.U. – U.S. Privacy Shield Framework and Swiss – U.S. Privacy Shield Framework. For more information please go to the section “International Transfers.”

This Privacy Statement includes some important information, please take the time to read the entire Privacy Statement. Of course, if you have any questions please reach out to us.
